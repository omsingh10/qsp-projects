Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hotel on IRCTC?
Enter your destination, check-in and check-out dates, select number of rooms and guests, then search for available hotels and complete your booking.
Can I get government rates for hotels?
Yes, government employees can avail special tariffs at empanelled hotels by selecting the 'Government Tariff' option while booking.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellation policy varies by hotel. Please check the specific hotel's cancellation terms before booking.
Are there any special discounts available?
Yes, we offer seasonal discounts, early bird offers, and special rates for government employees and senior citizens.
How do I modify my hotel booking?
Login to your account, go to 'My Bookings', select the reservation you want to modify and follow the modification process.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets for hotel bookings.
Can I book multiple rooms at once?
Yes, you can book multiple rooms in a single transaction by selecting the number of rooms in the search form.
Is breakfast included in hotel bookings?
It depends on the hotel and the room type you select. Check the hotel details for meal inclusions.
